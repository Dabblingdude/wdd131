Bridget's Keepsake Quilts & Bears Site Plan

Sam Niemand

WDD 131 - Section 1

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a place on the web for my mom to showcase and advertise her quilting products. She runs a business of selling customized quilts and bears, and we can use this site to help in that business. For now it will just be within a smaller scope for the sake of the class, but I will expand on it at a later date with her to improve productivity and functionality.

Audience

The audience for this website is any potential customers for my mom's business.

Dynamic elements

I will use JS to implement modals for the pictures of her past projects. I will use JS to make at least 1 image gallery where the users can click on arrows to view the next pictures. I will also make an extra simple subpage for a contact-me form submission for potential orders. If I figure it out, I will use JS to pull images from her FB page so that I can have it automatically update with the most recent projects. If I do that, I may be able to treat the posts as objects in an array. If not, I will do so with the main page's images and maybe make them random in order to an extent.
